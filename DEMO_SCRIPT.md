# 90-Second Demo Script — OilGas CRM

A tight stakeholder walkthrough. Total time ≈ 90 seconds.

---

**[0:00 — 0:10] Login**
> "This is OilGas CRM — a sales platform built for petroleum and
> petrochemical trading."

- On the login screen, click the **Admin** demo-account card, then **Sign In**.

**[0:10 — 0:25] Dashboard**
> "The moment you log in, you see the whole business — total sales, margins,
> active leads, and a live India sales heatmap."

- Point at the KPI strip and the sales-trend chart.
- Hover a state on the India map; click it to filter, then clear.

**[0:25 — 0:45] Leads & Pipeline**
> "Sales runs on leads. Here's the full list — colour-coded by how hot each
> lead is."

- Open **Leads**, then **Pipeline**.
- **Drag a card** from "Qualified" to "Negotiation" — "stages update instantly."

**[0:45 — 1:05] Proposal with GST**
> "When a lead is ready, we raise a proposal."

- **Proposals → Create Proposal**. Pick a customer, **Add Item** twice.
- "GST is automatic — same-state buyers get CGST+SGST, others get IGST."
- Click **Download PDF** — "a GST-compliant document, generated in the browser."

**[1:05 — 1:20] Role switching**
> "Different roles see different things."

- Top-right menu → **Switch Role → Accounts**.
- "The sidebar changes — Accounts focuses on invoices and payments."

**[1:20 — 1:30] Polish**
> "And it's a proper modern product."

- Press **⌘K** — show the command palette.
- Toggle **dark mode** from the top bar.

> "Everything you saw is live and clickable. Changes save in the browser,
> and a single click in Settings resets the demo."

---

### Tips
- Pre-open the app and dismiss the welcome splash before presenting.
- If anything looks off, **Settings → System → Reset Demo Data** restores it.
- The whole thing runs offline — no server, no internet needed.
